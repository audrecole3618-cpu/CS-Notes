# Binary & Data Representation
Computers don't see letters or colors; they see high and low voltages (1s and 0s).

### The Units of Measure
* **Bit:** A single 0 or 1.
* **Byte:** 8 bits. (The standard "building block" of data).
* **Hexadecimal (Hex):** Base-16. Used in InfoSec for memory addresses and color codes (like your purple theme!).

### Why it matters for InfoSec
* **Encoding vs. Encryption:** Encoding (like Base64) is for making data readable by different systems. Encryption is for hiding data from prying eyes. **Never confuse the two!**
