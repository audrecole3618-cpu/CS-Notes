# Networking Fundamentals
To secure a system, you have to know how it connects to the world.

### The OSI Model (The "Map" of the Internet)
1. **Physical:** Cables and hardware.
2. **Data Link:** Switches and MAC addresses.
3. **Network:** Routers and IP addresses.
4. **Transport:** TCP (Reliable) vs UDP (Fast).
5. **Session:** Managing connections.
6. **Presentation:** Data formatting (Encryption happens here!).
7. **Application:** What you see (HTTP, Email, Browsers).

> **Note:** Most InfoSec attacks happen at Layers 3, 4, and 7.
